Welcome To Family Life

At Bahamas Harvest Church, we desire to build boys and girls rather than repair men and women. We have three incredible age appropriate environments for your family: Harvest Adventurers (New Born - Pre-School); Kids Electric (Grades 1-6) and Crossover (Grades 7-12). Each of these environments create structured spiritual development, teaching students faith in God.
