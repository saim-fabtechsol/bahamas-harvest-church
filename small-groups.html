Small Groups

Life Is Better Together

Small Groups is all about creating environments for authentic Christian relationships to be experienced within communities. Everyone who walks through the doors of Bahamas Harvest Church has the opportunity to find a friend.

Sign-Up For A Small Group This Semester

R.E.A.L. Community

Our R.E.A.L  Community is where 18-24 year old young people do life together beyond the Sunday experience. Whether you are a college student, pursuing a career or searching, it is our desire to help you know Jesus and grow spiritually in a loving community of believers.

Eden Women's Community

Our Eden Women's Small groups are designed to meet and address the varying needs of a female as she transitions the various stages of life.  Our Eden’s women small groups have been described as pillars of strength while navigating this journey of life.  Based on your choosing, you’re able to glean from the wisdom of the golden pearls, while simultaneously being taught the latest technology vernacular from a millennial. Our Eden’s women community focuses on the whole woman; mind, body, spirit and soul.

Blueprint Men's Community

The Blueprint Men Small Groups are man-cave like environments where men come together in a group setting, with the opportunity to make life-long friendships, and grow spiritually with other men of God. Small Groups are open to both members and non-members of Bahamas Harvest Church.

Connected Married Life Community

Each Connected Married Life group is diverse in its make-up and design, encompassing newly weds, remarried couples, couples with and without children, and empty nesters.  All of whom are seeking to learn more about God, learn more about each other and develop strategies to solidify long lasting marriages.

Fusion Singles Community

The Fusion Singles Small Groups are designed for single adults to connect with God and each other in healthy Christian environments.  This community is designed for persons to glean from each others strengths and learnings as they traverse the various pathways of life together, with a biblical perspective.
